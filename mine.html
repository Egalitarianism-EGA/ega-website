Mine EGA

Three algorithms. Mine alone, with friends on the same network, or through a pool.

Pick your hardware

RandomXModern CPUs — best everyday option
YespowerEGAWeaker CPUs, older machines, Pi-class devices
VerthashGPUs — see the GPU miner guide on GitHub

Solo mining

You run a node and mine to your own wallet. Blocks you find pay you.

bash scripts/easy-start.sh
bash scripts/easy-mine.sh randomx
# or: bash scripts/easy-mine.sh yespower-ega

GPU: ega-verthash-miner

Pool mining

Connect your miner to a stratum pool and get paid by share of work — like other coins.

Pool details

Mine with friends (no pool)

Everyone runs a node and connects to each other. Same chain. Whoever finds a block keeps that reward. 

# on your machine (~/.ega/ega.conf)
addnode=FRIEND_IP:20201
