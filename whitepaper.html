Whitepaper

Egalitarianism (EGA) · v0.1 · fair-launch multi-algo PoW. Not financial advice.

1. Vision

Money and mining power should not require elite hardware or insider allocation.

  • Permissionless value transfer
  • Mining open to CPUs, GPUs, phones, and SBCs
  • No premine / founder stash
  • Resist single hardware-class capture

2. Principles

  1. Fair launch — genesis reward is zero
  2. Multi-door mining — four algorithms (MultiShield-4)
  3. Simple emission — Bitcoin-style halvings
  4. Independent difficulty — MultiShield per algo
  5. Honesty — capital still matters; design raises the bar for monopoly

3. Tokenomics
