Mining pool

Point a miner at stratum, use your EGA address as username, get paid shares on Verthash and Scrypt.

Live stratum

Verthash (GPU)stratum+tcp://105.225.100.58:3334
Scryptstratum+tcp://105.225.100.58:3336
UsernameYour EGA wallet address (starts with E)
Passwordx
Operator must leave the pool online and forward ports 3334 / 3336 for the internet to reach them. Dashboard (human UI): http://105.225.100.58:8089/ when port 8089 is open — or ask the operator.

GPU Verthash example

VerthashMiner -a verthash \
  -o stratum+tcp://105.225.100.58:3334 \
  -u YOUR_EGA_ADDRESS -p x \
  --all-cl-devices -f ega-verthash.dat \
  --no-verthash-data_verification

Dataset: ega-verthash-miner (EGA 256 MiB file, not stock Vertcoin).
